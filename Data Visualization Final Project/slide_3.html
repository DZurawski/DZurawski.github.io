A "Number of Moves" value of 0 indicates that the player did not castle during their game.

Hover over a data point to get more information.

The games used as samples consist of all blitz chess games played on FICS during 2016 such that for each game, the participants had an average Elo rating of 2000 or higher. Elo ratings represent how well players perform. Players are awarded 1 point for winning, 0.5 points for drawing and 0 points for losing.
